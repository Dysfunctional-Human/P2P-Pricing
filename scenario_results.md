## Key Findings

1. **SDR-DSM consistently achieves the highest fairness index** (F = 0.80–0.88) across all community sizes,
 confirming the mechanism's equity properties are not specific to the N=10 case.

2. **MMR and static SDR consistently penalize prosumers** (negative Δ_P) regardless of community size,
 validating the structural inequity identified in the main study.

3. **BSM offers moderate fairness but limited savings** — it never exceeds ~8% community savings
 and provides no temporal price signals for load shifting.

4. **The framework scales without structural modification** from 7 to 30 households,
 with settlement functions and pricing formulations unchanged.

5. **SDR-DSM is the only mechanism delivering both positive Δ_C and positive Δ_P simultaneously**,
 establishing Pareto-optimality that no other evaluated mechanism achieves.
